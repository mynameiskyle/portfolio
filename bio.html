My work lies at the intersection of business goals and a pleasurable user experience.

I'm a product and user experience designer with strong visual design skills. I have a firm grasp of the principles of good design. These principles, when applied to web and mobile products, make for engaging visual and user interface design.

After getting my BFA at Savannah College of Art and Design, I returned to my hometown of Pensacola and began working as a web designer. While at HailStudio, a local web shop, I was scouted by a local startup to help design their mobile chat application.

Since then I've been pouring my time into building products and experiences for local and national startups. Fpr the past three years I've been the Product Designer at Accountingfly, a job board and applicant tracking system for the accounting industry.
