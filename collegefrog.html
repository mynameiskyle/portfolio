CollegeFrog

Branding, UI/UX, Visuals, HTML, CSS

CollegeFrog is a service that matches accounting students with firms across the country. Students who use CollegeFrog benefit by keeping real-time data before prospective employers, while firms benefit by getting to simplify and streamline the recruiting process.

Visit the Site
One of the challenges with CollegeFrog was getting college students to build yet another online profile. Even though our service is valuable, there was still a lot of information we needed to capture.

To solve the problem, we segmented the signup process into sections and offered a profile progress bar to encourage students to complete the most importants parts of their profile. The parts the employers cared for the most.

Another challenge was having two audiences: students & employers.

Our solution was a dedicated page for each user type but focusing collegefrog.com on students. Employers have a call-to-action in the navigation to post a job. This was a typical workflow in other, similar sites, so we felt confident it was an intuitive path for our employer users.
